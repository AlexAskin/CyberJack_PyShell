CyberJACK for Python Shell

main file:
CyberJACK_pyShell_Version.py

Cyberjack - gambling card game (variant of Blackjack and Twenty-One)

HIT - Press '1' + 'Enter'
STAND - Press 'Enter'

____ MENU ____
0 - Show Menu
1 - Play Round!
2 - Change Bet
3 - Show Account Info
4 - Show Statistics
5 - "Get Five!" (mini-game) 
6 - Change Nickname

00 - Show Extended Menu
01 - Show Game Rules

9 - Exit

____EXTENDED MENU ____
11 - Get 1 Random Card
55 - Get 5 Random Cards
22 - Get a Few Nums of Random Cards
33 - Drop Random Cards Hand

98 - Show deck_list
99 - Show Cards Deck
